**Goal**
TMD-26631 Verify Fluid Flow Rate field properties in Cooling Unit.
---
## Test Steps
1. Go To Models Library
2. **Verify Fluid Flow Rate Column Is Hidden By Default**
3. Click Show/Hide Column
4. Select Fluid Flow Rate
5. Click Apply Button
6. **Verify Fluid Flow Rate Column Is Visibile In Models Library**
7. Go To Field Management Page
8. Click Standard Fields Subtab
9. Filter Fluid Flow Rate On Label
10. **Verify Fluid Flow Rate Is Visible**
